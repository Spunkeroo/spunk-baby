✓

✓
Fitted sheets — 2-3 minimum (blowouts happen)
✓
Swaddle blankets — muslin, 4-6 pack
✓
Sound machine — white noise for longer sleep
✓
Baby monitor — video + audio recommended

✓
Breast pump — if breastfeeding, get insured first
✓

✓

✓

✓

✓

New Parent Tips

What nobody tells you before the baby arrives

🛍️
Don't overbuy before baby arrives
Buy the basics, see what your specific baby likes. Every baby is different — some hate swings, some live in them. Wait to buy big items until you know what works.
📦
Amazon Subscribe & Save for diapers
Set up auto-delivery on Amazon for diapers and wipes — 15% off every order. You'll use thousands of diapers in the first year. Never run out at 3am again.
🩺
Babies outgrow things fast
Buy some secondhand gear (strollers, bouncers, clothes) but always buy NEW for car seats and crib mattresses — safety items should never be used.
💳
Set up a baby Amazon registry
Even if you don't need gifts — the registry completion discount gives you 15% off everything remaining after your shower. Free money for must-haves.
